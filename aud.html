Auditioning With Talanta.ke

The Talanta.ke is a owned and operated national organization with hand selected regional directors,dance centers, theater companies,

and more coming from all over the country with the top Agents, Managers and Casting Directors to offer a unique experience.

Aspiring actors, singers, dancers and models have their time stage performing on stages.

There's nothing else like it that offers such opportunity with the best the entertainment industry has to offer.Our goal is giving each aspiring young talent a first look at the world of entertainment.

Many compare their TALENT experience to a career day that lasts for entire week. This is a national networking!

Preparing for Auditons

1. Please wear age-appropriate, wholesome clothing that fits properly.

2. Bring a parent! If you’re under 18 years old you must have a parent with you to audition.

3. The minimum audition age is 7 years

4. Everyone is required to audition for acting and then you can choose one other audition (singing, dancing, modeling)

5. We don’t charge a fee for the auditions.
